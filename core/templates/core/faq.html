Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our DNA testing services.

Is this legal?
Yes, we provide personal and court-certified results. Our Legal DNA kit follows all chain-of-custody procedures required for legal proceedings.
Will anyone know I've taken a test?
No, all tests are coded with anonymous identifiers and delivered discreetly. Your privacy is our highest priority, and we maintain strict confidentiality protocols.
Can I test without the mother's knowledge or involvement?
Yes, with legal rights to the child, you can conduct testing without the mother's involvement. Our Peace of Mind kit is designed for discreet sample collection.
How accurate are the results?
Our tests are above 99.99% accurate. We work exclusively with ISO-certified laboratories that meet international standards for DNA testing.
How long does it take to get results?
Results are typically available within 3-5 business days after we receive your sample at our laboratory.
How are results delivered?
Results are delivered securely through our encrypted online portal. For legal tests, certified hard copies can also be provided upon request.
What if I need help collecting the sample?
Our kits include clear, step-by-step instructions. If you need additional assistance, our support team is available via phone or WhatsApp.
Can the results be used in court?
Only our Legal DNA kit results can be used in court proceedings. These tests follow strict chain-of-custody procedures required by the legal system.
Is my information secure?
Yes, we employ advanced security measures to protect your personal information and test results. All data is encrypted and stored securely.
