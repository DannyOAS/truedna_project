How TrueDNA Testing Works

Our simple, discreet process is designed to give you accurate results with complete privacy.

  1. Request Your Kit

    Order your testing kit online, by phone, or via WhatsApp. Choose between our Peace of Mind kit for personal knowledge or our Legal DNA kit for court-admissible results.

    Your kit will be delivered in discreet packaging with no company branding or indication of its contents.

  2. Collect Your Sample

    Follow the simple instructions included in your kit to collect cheek swab samples. The process is painless and takes less than 5 minutes to complete. Our kits are designed for easy self-collection in the privacy of your home.

    Each kit contains everything you need: sterile swabs, collection containers, detailed instructions, and return packaging.

  3. Return Your Sample

    Once you've collected your samples, seal them in the provided packaging according to the instructions. You can choose between our free pickup service or drop off your sample at one of our designated collection points.

    For Legal DNA tests, our trained staff will verify your identity and document the chain of custody necessary for court-admissible results.

  4. Receive Your Results

    Your samples are processed in ISO-certified laboratories using the latest DNA analysis technology. Results are typically available within 3-5 business days after your sample arrives at the lab.

    You'll receive a secure link to view your results online through our encrypted portal. For Legal DNA tests, certified hard copies can also be provided.

The Science Behind Our Tests

TrueDNA testing uses the latest in genetic science to analyze and compare DNA markers. Our laboratories examine specific locations in the DNA where variations occur between individuals. By comparing these genetic markers between the child and alleged father, we can determine biological relationships with greater than 99.99% accuracy.

All tests are performed in ISO-certified laboratories using a minimum of 21 genetic markers to ensure the highest level of reliability.
